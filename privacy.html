CoC-BoT Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 14 May 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what information CoC-BoT may collect, store, or process while operating on Discord servers.

CoC-BoT is a privately self-hosted Discord bot operated in the United Kingdom.

2. Information Collected

CoC-BoT may collect or store limited Discord-related information required for functionality, including:

  • Discord user IDs
  • Discord server IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Role IDs
  • Message IDs
  • Server configuration settings
  • Birthday information
  • Moderation or logging data
  • Virtual economy data where enabled

3. Message Content

CoC-BoT may temporarily process message content in order to:

  • Respond to commands
  • Perform moderation actions
  • Handle music or utility features
  • Execute bot-related actions

Message content is not permanently stored unless required by a specific feature such as moderation logging.

4. Third-Party Services

CoC-BoT may interact with third-party services including:

  • Discord
  • YouTube
  • Spotify

Those services may independently collect or process information according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Usage

Collected data is used solely for:

  • Operating bot functionality
  • Storing server preferences
  • Providing moderation or utility features
  • Maintaining stability and operation of the bot

Data is not sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for bot functionality.

Server owners and users may remove certain stored information through bot commands or by removing the bot from a server.

7. Security

Reasonable efforts are made to protect stored data. However, no system can be guaranteed fully secure.

As CoC-BoT is privately self-hosted, availability and security are maintained on a best-effort basis.

8. Children's Privacy

CoC-BoT is not intended for users under the minimum age required by Discord's Terms of Service.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time without prior notice.

Continued use of CoC-BoT after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
